Elementos de tu puesto de trabajo en inglés

Lee la siguiente conversación entre Veronica y Adam, sobre los elementos de su oficina. Repite si es necesario.

A tour of your new office space
Adam: Veronica! Welcome to the office.
Veronica: Hi Adam!
Adam: It’s nice to meet you in person.
Veronica: It is. Wow, the office looks great!
Adam: Yeah, Well, let me give you the official tour. This is your office.
Veronica: Nice!
Adam:
There is a printer
There is a laptop
There is a desk
There is a chair, of course!
Veronica: Yes.
Adam:
There is a keyboard
There are markers
There is a mouse
There are scissors
There is a cactus or a plant
There is a plant pot
And there is a computer monitor
Veronica: Well, thank you so much for the tour of the office, everything looks nice!
Adam: Of course, if you need anything, let me know.
Veronica: Thank you!

Control de lectura
What office elements can you remember?
a. A chair and a desk
b. A TV and a radio
c. A sofa and a printer

Which is NOT an office element?
a. A keyboard
b. A cactus or a plant
c. A sofa

Respuestas
A chair and a desk
A sofa
